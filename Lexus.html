Infomation

Exterior - Youthful, personality

In particular, the Q2 2021 version on display at the dealer has been equipped with an S-line equipment package with many eye-catching decorative details. The vehicle's overall dimensions are 4,210 x 1,790 x 1,550 mm, respectively, and the length is increased by 17 mm compared to its predecessor.

Wind resistance coefficient reached 0.31, the best parameter in the segment, contributing to improved acceleration.

In the international market, the front light cluster of the Audi Q2 2021 has been upgraded to matrix LED technology. It's a pity that the facelift of Q2 in Vietnam still uses regular LED lighting, accompanied by an automatic on and off feature and a lamp washing function.

Interior - Simple

Maybe many customers will feel disappointed when discovering the cabin of the Audi Q2 2021. Because of the simple design style, it has not brought a luxurious experience like the BMW X1 or Mercedes-Benz GLA.

It's a pity that the central screen remains the same size of 5.8 inches, too small to be able to meet the entertainment needs well. In return, the cabin compartment has better insulation than before thanks to the addition of heat-resistant glass on all side doors.
